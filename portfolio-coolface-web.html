CoolFace Website Project

The Project

CoolFace is a professional tent and camping equipment rental company. Their current online order system is separated into two part. One is using blog to display the products, and then user need to use another online registration system to reserve their order.

The goal of this web application is to integrate the process in one site to provide fluent shopping experience.

Go to CoolFace Website

Functions

For customers

Customer can browse product lists and details by categories. After adding product into shopping cart, rent will be calculate base by selected days and quantity, and will be updated real time after any change.

After reserving product, all the details about this order will be shown on the orders page, such payment status, shipping status, estimated shipping dates and shipping method. Deposit can also be pay on the order details page.

For Administrators

Dark theme is used for administrative pages to distinguish from customer pages.

Administrators can manage orders, categories, products, and news posts.
